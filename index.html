NEW GAME

HOW TO PLAY:
Fill all the the squares from 1 to 100 by following two simple rules:
  1. You can start in any cell you want
  2. Next numbers can be put anywhere too by skipping 2 squares vertically and horizontally or by skipping 1 square diagonally
